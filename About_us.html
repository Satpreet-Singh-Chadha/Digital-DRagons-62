About us and our goals

Our team consists of distinctive individuals who have contributed their part towards the success of this project. The prodigious members are Satpreet Singh Chadha, Sharvil Shetty, Sparsh Bhimrajka and Jash Shah.

We are the ambitious students of ppsijc who wish to contribute towards the betterment of the environment. We are strong believers that sustainable practices can make the earth a better place to live in. The health and well-being of the environment directly impact our own health and quality of life. Hence, the subject of protecting the environment is of paramount importance. We believe that this is a fundamental issue that challenges the human resource as a whole and requires the collective effort from governments, businesses, diplomats, and young minds like our very own.

We provide multiple services such as actionable steps to save the environment, a carbon footprint calculator, a graph of the recent carbon emission trends and the latest news on the environment to aid society as a whole in comprehending the importance of the environment, how they can contribute and recent developments in the environmental sector.
